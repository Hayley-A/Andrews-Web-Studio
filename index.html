A little bit about me.

I grew up in the piney woods of East Texas where I would dream of what I wanted to be when I grew up. It took 40 years to figure that out. I've done everything from managing a restaurant to bookkeeping ...finding enjoyment in whatever I was doing and working hard at it. But regardless of my career, nothing felt quite right.

Through a series of unlikely events, I discovered web development and I fell in love. I love that this career allows me the freedom to spend time with my husband and kids, while being rewarded with knowing that I'm adding a bit of common sense and beauty to the interwebs. I love everything about designing customized and intuitive websites. My calideoscope of experience serves me well as inspiration for design.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Converting Ideas into Stunning Designs

My clients inspire my website design to become the perfect extension of them from the color theme to element placement and ease of use.
